Why Sarah will never be forgotten:

  • She is Perfect - She was too good for this world so God took her immediately to be with Him.
  • Hope of the Resurrection - Even though my wife and I already knew about and had faith in Jesus Christ and his victory over death, knowing that Sarah is waiting for us gives us incredible hope.
  • She is Ours for Eternity - Because Sarah was born in the covenant by virtue of my wife and I having been sealed in the temple by restored priesthood authority, she will be with us for time and all eternity. We are separated now but it is only a see you later.
